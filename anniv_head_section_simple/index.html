Zwaye 4-an mariaz

Zot Lamour li briye, kouma enn labouzi ki pa tengn zame. 4 an mariaz, sak zour kinn pase, sak lane enn saler dan zot leker, ki fer zot kontign eklere. La sir ki koule, kouma letan ki pase, li rapel zot pasians, zot devouman. Mem kan divan soufle, zot laflam res vivan, parski gras a lamour zot inn konstrir enn paravan. alor si labouzi lasir donn lalimier, mo sir zot lamour kapav eklere a vi.
